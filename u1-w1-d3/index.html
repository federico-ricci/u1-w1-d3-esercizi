focaccia with caramelized onions, cramberries, and brie

wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. it will become your new favorite holiday starter!

fresh cranberry recipes that go beyound sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-pourous flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukerwarm water
  • 1 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onion, thily sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 onces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
